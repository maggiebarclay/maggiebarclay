Computer Discovery Camp

Computer Discovery Camp aims to encourage and excite middle school girls to learn more about computers. As an instructor, I helped to create and teach cirricula for two sessions of the camp. The lesson plans allowed students to begin recognizing and utilizing basic programming concepts as they on Lego Mindstorm robots that they built. They were also taught the functions of a database, learning how to create, read, and update relational databases. This emphasized the importantance and methods of storing large amounts of data.

Being an instructor at CDC allowed me to spread my excitement for science and technology with young girls, something I'm passionate about being a woman in STEM. I hope to make a positive impact by encouraging other girls to pursue interests in STEM.
