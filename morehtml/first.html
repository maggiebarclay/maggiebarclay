FIRST Robotics

FIRST (For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology) is a global organization that inspires youth to explore STEM through robotics competitions. Joining my team in high school (Ironclad, team 5847) sparked my interest in STEM, and I gained important technical and leadership skills.

As team Captain, I oversaw the robot design and build process. Throughout my years on the team, I created and managed public STEM outreach activities and sponsorship opportunities with local companies. I worked on both technical (mechanical, electrical) and non-technical (marketing, branding), and mentored younger students in these areas. I continue to be involved with FIRST by volunteering at competitions.
